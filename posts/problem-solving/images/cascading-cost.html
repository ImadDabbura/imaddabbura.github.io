⚠️ Key Insight

A seemingly minor 10% misunderstanding in the problem definition phase doesn't just add 10% to your project cost. It compounds through each subsequent phase, potentially resulting in 80% waste by deployment—or complete project failure. This is why Polya emphasized: "Understanding the problem is half the solution."
